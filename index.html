Abrar Anwar

I'm Abrar! I'm a senior in Computer Science at the University of Texas at Austin (Class of 2021)

At UT, I primarily conduct research with the Building-Wide Intelligence Project with Dr. Justin Hart Here, I gained experience in robotics, localization, navigation, perception, and manipulation working on numerous projects over the years.

These last two summers, I have been interning at Sandia National Laboratories, where I have been working with the Neural Exploration and Research Lab. In 2019, I worked with Dr. James Aimone on brain-inspired navigation and rotation-invariance for a hypersonic glide vehicle. In 2020, I have been working with Dr. Craig Vineyard to evolve sparse, nosie resilient, spiking neural network topologies.
